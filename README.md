# HR Cheat Sheet PWA

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
[![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.3.0-blue.svg)](https://github.com/WorldOfSitesNet/hr-interview-pwa/releases)

A professional Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help job seekers prepare for interviews by managing common questions and answers in multiple languages. Perfect for those targeting international companies and practicing English/Ukrainian interviews simultaneously.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Dual-Language Workflow**: Manage questions and answers in English and Ukrainian side-by-side.
- **Mobile Optimized**: Responsive design with a smart adaptive navbar for seamless use on any screen size.
- **Dynamic Management**: Add, edit, and delete questions with real-time updates.
- **Drag & Drop**: Intuitive reordering of questions to prioritize your study plan.
- **Data Portability**: Full Export/Import functionality (JSON) to keep your data across different devices.
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally in your browser's `LocalStorage`. No servers, no tracking.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on iOS, Android, and Desktop with offline support.
- **Adaptive UI**: High-contrast Dark and Light modes with Bootstrap 5.

## üöÄ How to Use

1. **Add Question**: Click the "+" button and fill in fields for both UA and EN versions.
2. **Categorize**: Use the "Categories" manager to group questions by job titles (e.g., "General", "QA", "Management").
3. **Organize**: Use the vertical grip icon to drag and drop questions into your preferred order.
4. **Sync**: Regularly use the **Export** feature (found in the gear menu) to back up your data to a file.
5. **Install**: Use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" to use the app like a native mobile tool.
